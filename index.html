    An unlikely prodigy

    As a child, Nahomi would follow her older sister to soccer practice and entertained herself by picking up skills on the sideline.

    Her dedication soon led her to become the star of the youth league, performing at the level of girls twice her age, not letting her small size keep her back.

    “I was shocked that a girl could be this good.”
    Megumi Kamionobe Midfielder No.10 Japanese National Team

    The perfect duo

    Nahomi and Megumi Kamionobe, her best friend and teammate, combined to score 70% of their teams goals. Nahomi has compared their success to that of Tsubasa and Misaki of the popular soccer manga, Captain Tsubasa.

    An International success

    Both Nahomi and Megumi would go on to play for the Japanese National team, winning gold for the first time in the nation's history at the 2011 World Cup, as well as silver at both the 2012 London Olympics and the 2015 World Cup.
